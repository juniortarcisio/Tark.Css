Present Continous

Objective

Explain and give some examples of Present Continous Tense, using regular verbs

Use Cases

  • The action is happening on the speech time
  • The action is temporary

The general rule is to just apply the auxiliar "to be" temporary/situational verb "Estar" plus another verb. Which means a temporary or short/medium term situation.

Examples

Statement Reason
Ela está viajando Happening, Temporary
Vocês estão felizes hoje Happening, Temporary
Ela está comendo Happening, Temporary
