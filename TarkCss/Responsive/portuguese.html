Verbs conjugation - Simple present

These following are the both main verbs (To be in it's two Portuguese forms) and some regular verbs in the simple present form of the Portuguese Language

To be verb - Ser

Portuguese Verb: "Ser". It means a permanent or long therm state of to be, for example I'm a man, I'm a human, I'm a student

English Noun Verb
I (Myself) Eu Sou
You (Singular) Você É
He (Singular) Ele É
She (Singular) Ela É
It/This (Singular/Near) Isto É
It/That (Singular/Far) Aquilo É
You (Plural) Vocês São
They/He (Plural/Male) Eles São
They/She (Plural/Female) Elas São
It/These (Plural/Near) Estes São
It/Those (Plural/Far) Aqueles São
We (Ourselves) Nós Somos
