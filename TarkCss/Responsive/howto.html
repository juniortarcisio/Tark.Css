How to Learn

Objective

To give some tips about how to start learning a new language

Initial Practices

  • Basic expressions
  • Basic vocabulary (speech, listeen)
  • Basic grammar
  • Practice through exercises
  • Practice through flashcards

Intermediate practices

  • Intermediate vocabulary (speech, listeen)
  • Intermediate grammar
  • Practice through exercises
  • Practice through flashcards
  • Try to begin practice on Whatsapp groups

Advanced practices

  • Advanced vocabulary (speech, listeen)
  • Advanced grammar
  • Practice through exercises
  • Practice through flashcards
  • Practice on Whatsapp and Skype groups
  • Often listen podcasts or watch Videos (Series, Youtube, News) of your interest

Fluency level

  • Practice through exercises
  • Practice through flashcards
  • Practice on Whatsapp and Skype groups
  • Often listen podcasts or watch Videos (Series, Youtube, News) of your interest
  • Take it easy on yourself, you won't have native skills, unless you live as a native for sometime
  • Don't worry too much about your accent, the priority is to understand and to be understood
  • Even about understanding, you will always find a new accent, new local expressions and don't let it disapoint you, it's absolutely normal and acceptable
