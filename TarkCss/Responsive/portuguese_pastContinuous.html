Past Continous

1. Objective

Explain and give some examples of Past Continous Tense, using regular verbs

2. Use Cases

  • The action has already been finished
  • The action happened in a range of time in the past
  • Pretty similar to present continous
  • As in English Language, usually it's used to tell something that happened between a range of time from another action
  • Mostly used together "when"/"quando"
  • Example: I was walking when I saw him. Eu estava andando quando eu vi ele.
