Verbs conjugation - Simple past - Regular verbs

Regular verbs are verbs which follow a same pattern when conjugating, following there are some examples

On past conjugating, we can divide it's logic into two patterns:

  • Verbs that ends with "E(R)"
  • Verbs that ends with any other vowel different that "E" (plus R) as A, I, O, U (plus R)
