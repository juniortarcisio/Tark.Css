Simple Past

1. Objective

Explain and give some examples of Simple Past Tense, using regular verbs

2. Use Cases

  • The action has already been finished
  • The action time is defined or undefined (For portuguese, different than English which Simple Past contemplates undefined time only)

3. Grammar table

On Portuguese past conjugating, there are two patterns

Regular verbs that ends with:
  • AR, example "andar", "nadar"
  • ER, IR example "comer", "beber", "comer", "pedir"

Mostly of portuguese verbs ends with AR and ER, sometimes IR, dont worry about exceptions now

4. Examples

Verbs that ends with AR

Statement Reason
Eu nadei ontem, então andei pouco Finished Defined Time
Ele estudou muito e ganhou uma boa nota Finished Undefined Time
Nós falamos com ele, mas ele não entendeu Finished Undefined Time
Elas trabalharam mais cedo, e ficaram cansadas Finished Defined Time

Verbs that ends with ER, IR

Statement Reason
Eu comi bastante Finished Undefined Time
Ele pediu dinheiro para mim Finished Undefined Time
Vocês queriam viajar a semana passada Finished Defined Time
Aqueles garotos aprenderam muito Português Finished Undefined Time
