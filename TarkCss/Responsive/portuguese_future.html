Simple Future

1. Objective

Explain and give some examples of Simple Future Tense, for any verb

2. Use Cases

  • The action will happens
  • The action is a certain or uncertain future
  • The action is a near or far future
  • Usually is complemented by a time description like now, in one hour, at 6pm, tomorrow, next week, next month, etc...

The auxiliar verb "Ir" can be used in two forms:

Vou, vai, vão, vamos

  • Informally used to describe any form of future
  • Formally used to describe a future that is nearly present as "Eu vou para casa agora", "I am going to home now"

Irei, Irá, Irão, Iremos

  • Informally not used
  • Formally used to describe a future not so close or so certain "Eu irei para casa as 9 horas", "I will go to home 9pm"

*The most of people ignore these rules above and just use "vou, vai vão, vamos" always, so I personally recommend beginner foreigners to just learn basics of "irei, irá, irão, iremos" and do not worry about using it at beginning, you will be well understood, then at a higher language level you can investigate Portuguese 1001 details that even natives don't understand well...

4. Examples

Vou, vai, vão, vamos

Statement Reason
Eu vou trabalhar agora, até logo Future close action
Eu vou trabalhar amanhã Future action informal
Ele vai viajar em setembro Future action informal
Aquelas garotas vão para o parque Future action informal
Nós vamos para a praia no fim de semana Future action informal

Irei, irá, irão, iremos

Statement Reason
Eu irei trabalhar amanhã Future action formal
Ele irá jogar bola mais tarde Future action formal
Elas irão andar juntas Future action formal
Aquelas pessoas irão para o Brasil amanhã Future action formal
Nós iremos estudar um pouco mais Future action formal
