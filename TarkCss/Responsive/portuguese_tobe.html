To Be Verb

These are the both main verbs ("To be") in the simple present form of the Portuguese Language

The object is to demonstratate the main nouns (actors) of a new language (I, You, She, He, etc...)

To be verbs fit on the situations

  • "Ser" is used to talk about what something "is", about being something or about some quality (adjective)
  • "Estar" is used for Present Continuous on Porguese language, it's a temporary form of "to be" verb

To be verb - Ser

"Ser" means a permanent or long term state of "to be" verb
For example: "I'm a human" (Permanent), "I'm a manager" (Long term)

Examples of application

  • Eu sou bonito (adjective)
  • Ele é Brasileiro (being Brazilian)
  • Vocês são legais (adjective)
  • Ela é gerente (begin a manager)

To be verb - Estar

"Estar" means a temporary or short/medium term state of "to be" verb
For example: "I'm sick" (temporary), I'm working in Brazil(short/medium term)
