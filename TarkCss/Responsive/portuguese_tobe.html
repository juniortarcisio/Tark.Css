To Be Verb

Objective

There are two main verbs ("To be") in the Portuguese Language: "Ser" and "Estar"

The object of "To be" verbs is to demonstratate the most used subjects (actors) of a new language (I, You, She, He, etc...) and the most used verb "To be"

Use Cases

  • "Ser" is used to talk about what something "is" on long term or permanently, about being something or about some quality (adjective)
  • "Estar" is used for Present Continuous on Portuguese language, it's a temporary or situational form of being something or having some quality

Examples of application (Permanent/long term)

  • Eu sou bonito (handsome: adjective)
  • Ele é Brasileiro (being Brazilian)
  • Vocês são legais (cool: adjective)
  • Ela é gerente (being a manager)
  • Nós somos altos (tall: adjective)
