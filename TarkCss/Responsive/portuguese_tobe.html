To Be Verb

1. Objective

The objective of "To be" verbs is to demonstratate the most used subjects (actors) of a new language (I, You, She, He, etc...), that are required to understand the next Verbal Tenses.
Also, to explain the differente between two main verbs ("To be") in the Portuguese Language: "Ser" and "Estar"

2. Use Cases

Ser

It's used to talk about what something "is" on long term or permanently, it's the Simple Present "To Be"

Estar

It's used to talk about what something "is" on temporary or situationally, when followed by a verb it represents Present Continous. It's a point of difference from the English Language, in Portuguese it's possible to specify when you are talking about a temporary or situational state using the "To be" verb "Estar".

4. Examples

Ser

Statement Reason
Ele é Brasileiro Nationality: Permanent
Eu sou bonito Appearance: Long term
Vocês são legais Attitude: Long term
Ela é gerente Job: long term
Nós somos altos Attribute: permanent

Estar

Statement Reason
Eu estou bonito hoje Today: Temporary
Vocês estão felizes hoje Today: Temporary
Nós estamos cansados State: temporary

When it's followed by a verb, it's present continuos

Statement Reason
Ela está viajando Happening, Temporary
