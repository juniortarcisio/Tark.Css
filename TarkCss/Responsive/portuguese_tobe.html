To Be Verb

1. Objective

There are two main verbs ("To be") in the Portuguese Language: "Ser" and "Estar"

The objective of "To be" verbs is to demonstratate the most used subjects (actors) of a new language (I, You, She, He, etc...) and the most used verb "To be"


2. Use Cases

  • "Ser" is used to talk about what something "is" on long term or permanently
  • "Estar" is used to talk about what something "is" on temporary or situationally, when followed by a verb it represents Present Continous

4. Examples

Ser - Examples

Statement Permanent, Long term
Ele é Brasileiro being: Permanent
Eu sou bonito context long term
Vocês são legais context long term
Ela é gerente job long term
Nós somos altos being: permanent

Estar - Examples

Statement Temporary, situational
Eu estou bonito hoje handsome: temporary
Vocês estão felizes hoje happy: situational
Nós estamos cansados tired: temporary

When it's followed by a verb, it's present continuos:

Statement Temporary, situational
Ela está viajando traveling: Present continuous action
